\subsubsection{Phase 5}
When the open well has collapse the last phase is entered. In this phase the remaining $\sum M_w - 2$ blocks are placed. The purpose of this phase is to both clear the remaning black cells in column 4, and clear the remaining white cells in column 3 and 4 by making them meet.
